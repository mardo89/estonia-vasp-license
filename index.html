Estonia requirements at a glance

Estonia has the most substance-heavy crypto regime in the EU. Requirements include local director, physical office, MLRO, and significant own funds.

  • Corporate vehicle: Estonian OÜ (Osaühing — private limited) with €2,500 minimum share capital. Mandatory.
  • Paid-up capital: €100,000 minimum for VASP. Higher for some activities.
  • Own funds: €125,000 (light activity) to €350,000 (full exchange + custody). Maintained throughout operations.
  • Local Estonian director: At least 1 board member must be Estonian resident or have meaningful Estonian presence.
  • Physical office: Real Estonian office (not virtual). Inspection-ready.
  • MLRO: Estonia-based, with relevant qualifications (CAMS, ICA, or equivalent).
  • AML program: 5AMLD/6AMLD compliant; RAB-aligned with detailed risk assessment.
  • RAB-approved IT/security framework: Documented cybersecurity, custody segregation, business continuity.

Glossary: key terms for Estonia crypto setup

If you're new to crypto licensing in Estonia, these are the terms you'll encounter most often.

RAB
Rahapesu Andmebüroo — Estonian Financial Intelligence Unit. The competent authority for VASP/CASP authorization.
OÜ
Osaühing — Estonian private limited company. The standard corporate vehicle for VASP/CASP. €2,500 minimum share capital.
MiCA
Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation — EU framework. Estonia must transition all crypto businesses to CASP by 30 June 2026.
Own funds
Regulatory capital reserve held throughout operations. Estonia requires €125K (light) to €350K (full exchange + custody) on top of paid-up share capital.
MLRO
Money Laundering Reporting Officer — named individual responsible for AML compliance. Must be Estonia-based.
Substance
Real local presence — physical office, local staff, local director. Estonia tightened substance rules in 2022 after high-profile crypto failures.
Grandfathering
Transitional period (30 Dec 2024 – 30 Jun 2026) during which pre-MiCA Estonian VASPs can continue while transitioning to CASP.
CASP
Crypto-Asset Service Provider — MiCA-era term replacing VASP. EU passport across 27 member states.

Estonia crypto license — frequently asked questions

The most common questions from founders considering Estonia for their crypto company in 2026.

Is the Estonia VASP license still available in 2026?

Limited. New Estonian VASP authorizations are still issued, but RAB applies stricter substance scrutiny since 2022. From 30 December 2024, MiCA CASP authorization is the standard pathway for new applicants. Existing VASPs have until 30 June 2026 to transition to CASP under grandfathering.

Why did so many crypto companies leave Estonia in 2022–2024?

Estonia tightened the VASP regime substantially after several high-profile failures. Changes included: higher own funds (€125K–€350K), mandatory local director, physical office, and RAB approval of all directors and UBOs. Many lighter operators migrated to Lithuania (more flexible MiCA implementation). Estonia remains viable for operators willing to commit real substance.

Estonia VASP vs Lithuania CASP — which should I choose in 2026?

Lithuania CASP is the default for most new applicants in 2026: lower substance requirements, deeper crypto-friendly banking ecosystem, faster authorization. Estonia still wins for operators with genuine Estonian presence (Estonian founder, Tallinn office, local team) and brands that benefit from Estonia's pioneer-jurisdiction credibility. Both grant the same EU MiCA passport.

How much does Estonia VASP cost in 2026?

Total setup runs $30,000–$70,000 in year one excluding capital — €10,000 RAB fee, $15K–$30K AML program, $10K–$30K local substance (director + office), $5K–$15K legal. PLUS €100K paid-up capital + €125K–€350K own funds (held, not consumed). Annual maintenance $20K–$40K.

What is the Estonia 0% retained-earnings tax?

Estonia uniquely taxes corporate profits only when distributed (as dividends), not when earned. Retained earnings face 0% Estonian corporate tax. When distributed, dividends are taxed at 22% (since 2025). This makes Estonia attractive for crypto businesses reinvesting profits — capital can compound without annual tax drag.

Can I keep my Estonian VASP and operate from another country?

No. Estonia requires meaningful local substance. Operating an Estonian VASP entirely from outside Estonia triggers RAB action and risks revocation. If you can't commit Estonian substance, migrate to Lithuania CASP (lower substance bar) or Cyprus CASP. We handle the migration.

What's the Estonia VASP application process?

Five stages over 3–6 months: (1) Estonian OÜ formation (1–2 weeks), (2) capital deposit + own funds confirmation (2–4 weeks), (3) AML program build + local director appointment (4–8 weeks), (4) RAB application submission, (5) RAB review (60–120 business days). Banking runs in parallel from week 4.
