Estonia VASP/CASP Application Process 2026: 6-Stage Timeline

Estonia VASP/CASP authorization takes 3–6 months end-to-end. Here's the full 6-stage process.

The 6-stage Estonia VASP/CASP authorization process

Stages run partly in parallel. Strategy + OÜ formation kick off Day 1; capital + substance + AML build run weeks 2-12; RAB review weeks 12-30.

Stage 1: Strategy + substance commitment (Week 1)

You contribute: 30-minute call. Describe crypto activity, target customers, expected volumes, current corporate structure. Critical: validate that Estonian substance is feasible — can you commit to a local Estonian director, Tallinn office, and Estonia-based MLRO?

We deliver: Honest assessment. If Estonian substance is unworkable, we'll recommend Lithuania CASP (lighter substance bar, same MiCA passport). If Estonia fits, engagement letter + fixed-fee quote + project timeline.

Honest framing: About 60% of inquiries for Estonia VASP turn out to be better-suited to Lithuania CASP because the Estonian substance bar is too high. We'll route you correctly even if it means losing the Estonia engagement.

Stage 2: Estonian OÜ formation (Week 1-2)

We deliver: Estonian OÜ (Osaühing — private limited) formation with €2,500 share capital. Notary execution (can be done remotely with apostilled signatures or via Estonian e-Residency). Centre of Registers filing. Tallinn registered address provided. 1-2 weeks.

You contribute: Provide founder/director identification documents. Sign incorporation documents (digital signature via Estonian e-Residency simplifies this dramatically; otherwise apostilled paper signatures).

Tip — Estonian e-Residency: If you don't already have it, applying for Estonian e-Residency (~€100, 4-6 weeks) before Stage 2 dramatically simplifies all subsequent stages. e-Residency lets you sign documents digitally, manage the OÜ remotely, and complies with substance signaling.

Stage 3: Capital deposit + own funds (Week 2-6)

We deliver: Open Estonian bank account at LHV, SEB, Citadele, or Coop Pank. Coordinate auditor confirmation of capital deposit (mandatory before RAB submission).

You contribute: Wire €100,000 paid-up capital + €125,000-€350,000 own funds (depending on activity scope) to the Estonian OÜ's bank account. Source-of-funds documentation for the wire.

Banking is the bottleneck. Estonian banks have tightened crypto-business onboarding significantly since 2023. Expect 4-8 weeks for account opening at traditional Estonian banks. Some EMIs (Wise Business, certain crypto-friendly EMIs) are faster but may not accept the capital deposit. Plan around banking carefully.

Stage 4: Substance + AML setup (Weeks 4-12, parallel)

This is where Estonia's premium cost lives. Stage 4 has four parallel work streams:

4a. Local Estonian director appointment

At least 1 board member must be Estonian resident or have meaningful Estonian presence. Options:

  • Founder relocates to Estonia — best signal but requires personal commitment
  • Hire Estonian director ($30K-$80K/year full-time)
  • Fractional Estonian director — vetted introductions through X24Consulting ($8K-$30K/year)
  • Estonian e-Resident founder — counts as substance for some interpretations (verify case-by-case)

4b. Tallinn office

Real physical office in Tallinn (virtual addresses rejected by RAB). Options:

  • Shared office / coworking with dedicated workspace ($3K-$8K/year)
  • Private office in Tallinn business district ($10K-$30K/year)
  • Combined with director's home office if Estonian director located there (acceptable if professional setup)

4c. Estonia-based MLRO

Money Laundering Reporting Officer must be physically based in or regularly present in Estonia. Options:

  • Internal MLRO ($40K-$80K/year full-time hire)
  • Fractional MLRO from specialist firm ($5K-$15K/year)
  • Founder serves as MLRO if CAMS-certified and Estonia-based

4d. AML/KYC program build

5AMLD/6AMLD-compliant program with RAB-aligned procedures. Includes:

  • Customer Due Diligence (CDD/EDD) policies
  • Risk assessment specific to your business
  • Transaction monitoring rules
  • FIU reporting workflow
  • Staff training program
  • Annual independent review schedule

Stage 5: RAB submission (Week 12-14)

We deliver: Submit complete VASP/CASP application to RAB with €10,000 fee. Application typically 150-300 pages including:

  • Estonian OÜ incorporation documents + capital confirmation
  • Business plan with 3-year financial projections
  • AML/KYC program (60-120 pages)
  • IT and security framework
  • Business continuity plan
  • Outsourcing arrangements
  • Service descriptions
  • Customer-facing terms of service
  • Fit-and-proper documentation for all key personnel (~15-20 pages per person × 3-7 persons)
  • UBO disclosure for all owners ≥10%
  • Source-of-funds for capital deposit

Stage 6: RAB review + authorization (Weeks 14-30)

RAB has 60-120 business days to review. The process:

  1. Completeness check (1-2 weeks): RAB confirms application is complete. Incomplete applications get a deficiency letter with response deadline.
  2. Substantive review (8-20 weeks): Detailed review of business plan, AML program, IT framework, fit-and-proper, capital adequacy.
  3. Key personnel interviews (sometimes): RAB may request interviews with directors and MLRO.
  4. RFIs (Request for Information): Common throughout review. Respond within 7-14 days; slow responses extend timeline.
  5. Decision: Authorize, conditionally authorize, or refuse.

Total review: 60-120 business days (12-24 weeks). Most successful applications are at the longer end given Estonia's tightened scrutiny since 2022.

What can speed up the process

  • Estonian e-Residency — apply early, dramatically simplifies document signing
  • Capital ready before kick-off — €100K + €125-350K available immediately saves 2-4 weeks
  • Existing AML program from another EU jurisdiction — can be adapted to Estonia in days vs built from scratch (€10K-€20K savings)
  • Pre-vetted Estonian MLRO + director — X24Consulting introduces vetted Estonian providers, saves 4-8 weeks of recruitment
  • Lower-risk activity — light VASP (no custody) reviews faster than full VASP

What slows the process

  • Slow capital deposit — Estonian banking has tightened; 4-8 weeks for traditional bank account opening
  • Substance arrangements — finding qualified Estonian director + MLRO takes time
  • RAB RFI cycles — slow responses add 2-6 weeks per cycle
  • UBO complexity — multi-layered ownership requires additional disclosures and explanations
  • High-risk activities — privacy coins, mixers, sanctioned-jurisdiction nexus trigger extended scrutiny

FAQ — Estonia application process

How long does Estonia VASP/CASP authorization take in 2026?

3-6 months end-to-end. Pre-submission stages (OÜ formation, capital deposit, AML program, substance setup) take 2-3 months. RAB review takes 60-120 business days (12-24 weeks). Best case: 3-4 months for Estonian founders with e-Residency and capital ready. Typical: 4-6 months for foreign founders. Complex/full-exchange: 6-9 months.

Do I need to travel to Estonia for the application?

Not strictly required for the application itself, but recommended as a substance signal. Document signing can be done via Estonian e-Residency (digital signing) or apostilled paper signatures. RAB may request key personnel interviews — these can be done by video. Most successful CASP holders eventually establish some Estonian presence (MLRO, operations staff, occasional in-person meetings) for substance reasons.

What's the RAB review process like?

RAB reviews under MLTFPA: completeness check (1-2 weeks) → substantive review (8-20 weeks) → key personnel interviews (sometimes) → decision. RFIs (Request for Information) are common throughout. Total review: 60-120 business days. Most successful applications are at the longer end given Estonia's tightened scrutiny since 2022.

Can I start operations before RAB authorization is granted?

No. Estonian VASP/CASP services to clients require RAB authorization first. Operating without authorization triggers RAB enforcement. You CAN operate from another jurisdiction during the wait (e.g., Panama or Canada MSB for non-EU customers), and use the wait period to build technology, recruit, and prepare for launch.

What if RAB refuses authorization?

If RAB refuses, you receive a written decision with reasons. You can appeal to the Tallinn Administrative Court within 30 days, or address the deficiencies and re-apply. Most refusals can be addressed with corrected documentation or improved substance. Common refusal reasons: insufficient AML program detail, key personnel failing fit-and-proper, inadequate Estonian substance, capital concerns. Better to invest in a high-quality first application than to expect to refile.
